Toyota

Toyota Motor Corporation is a Japanese multinational automotive manufacturer founded in 1937. It is one of the world's largest automakers, known for its diverse range of vehicles, including sedans, SUVs, trucks, and hybrids. Toyota is a pioneer in hybrid technology, with the Prius being a notable model. The company has a global presence, emphasizing innovation, efficiency, and sustainability. Toyota is also the parent company of Lexus and has a stake in Daihatsu and Hino Motors.
