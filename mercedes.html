Mercedes

Mercedes-Benz is a German multinational automotive brand and a division of Daimler AG. Founded in 1926, Mercedes-Benz is renowned for luxury vehicles, buses, coaches, and trucks. The company is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany. Mercedes-Benz is a symbol of premium quality, cutting-edge technology, and innovative design. The brand offers a diverse range of vehicles, including sedans, SUVs, coupes, convertibles, and performance-oriented AMG models. Mercedes-Benz has a global presence and is recognized for its commitment to safety, comfort, and a rich heritage in the automotive industry.
